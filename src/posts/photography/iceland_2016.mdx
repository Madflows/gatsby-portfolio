In some ways, visiting Iceland was spontaneous. [Jeremy](http://partspermillion.nyc/) and I had been casually discussing the idea for nearly a year, but we finally pulled the trigger in April – only a few weeks in advance of the trip. We had little time to do more than hastily rent a camper van and plot a few dozen points on a Google Map. The uncertainty made for an even more exciting adventure though – giving us seemingly endless surprises while pushing forward on the Ring Road.
